Hello and welcome to U R Web!

A map free to use when you need to mark a certain place or to see if any of your friends are around. Or how close you are to one of your favourite locations.

You can find your way around a new city with the help of U R Web, all you have to do is sign up or log into your account and the website will provide information about the nearest points of interest like caffe's, bars, markets and so on.

The slideshow is a sample of how the website provides information.
